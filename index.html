App Design

Chefsy

Chefsy is an app that will cut down on the time and stress it takes to plan, shop for, and cook meals. It can generate a meal with the items you already have in you pantry. You can then shop the ingredients needed through the app with your favorite grocery store. You can also create a meal plan for the week to help you prepare for your next meal. It makes preparing a meals quick, easy, and fun.
